## DESIGN & CONVERSION NOTES FOR DEVELOPER

- **Color Palette Suggestion:** Deep navy (#0A1F44) + clean white + a single warm accent (gold or teal) for CTAs
- **Hero Background:** Professional headshot of Kyle on the right, copy stacked left — this is a personal authority brand; the face matters
- **Font Hierarchy:** Bold serif or clean sans-serif for headlines; open, readable sans for body
- **Mobile-First:** Form and CTAs must be full-width and thumb-friendly on mobile — expect majority of traffic from phones
- **Above the Fold:** Headline, subheadline, trust bar, and primary CTA must all be visible without scrolling on desktop
- **Social Proof Placement:** Insert testimonials near the bottom of the Authority section and just above the final form — this is the highest-trust placement
- **Form UX:** Minimize friction — keep required fields to: Name, Phone, Email, State. All others optional.
- **Page Speed:** Single-page, minimal JavaScript. This page should load in under 2 seconds.
- **SEO Title Tag:** `Health Insurance Broker | KJM Health | kylecoverage.com`
- **Meta Description:** `Get a free custom health insurance quote from Kyle Miller — a licensed broker in 26 states. Real guidance, no pressure. Marketplace & private plans compared for you.`
